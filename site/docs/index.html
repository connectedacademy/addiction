Connected Learning

This course realises a new type of Connected Learning, giving students the opportunity to share knowledge and experience with peers, photographers and experts across the globe.

Peer Feeback

During the course there will be opportunities to create photographs with respect to the course material and receive feedback from peer students to help develop your work.
